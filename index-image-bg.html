About Us

Agrigate is a pioneering agri-tech startup working on leveraging technology to provide advisory and marketplace solutions to a wide range of stakeholders in the agricultural sector in Bangladesh.

We are committed to the sustainable development of rural communities, mainly farmers and rural entrepreneurs by utilizing technology to revolutionize information delivery, input sourcing and output sales supported by our Agrigate platform which consists of multifaceted service delivery channels, augmenting in person services delivered via our strategically located Agrigate centers with our proprietary app and web-based services.

In order to implement this bold vision, Agrigate is engaged in building a robust supply chain network by working with companies who espouse our ethos of quality over profits thereby building an enriched ecosystem for all stakeholders in our platform.

We believe that as a result, our platform will become an indispensable medium for farmers and rural entrepreneurs to connect with each other, with companies and the wider agricultural community to engage in various economic activities. To this end, Agrigate is working with farmers, input sellers, rural entrepreneurs, companies and business partners to contribute to the development of a rich ecosystem for the users of our platform.
