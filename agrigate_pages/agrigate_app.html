Agrigate App

Agrigate app is a networking app for the rural agriculture community where all the stakeholders can access to a range of services.


The stakeholders our app has been designed are for:

1. Farmers
2. Local Traders
3. Local Agri-Input Retailers
4.Service Providers such as Livestock service providers and Agri-machinery service provider.
